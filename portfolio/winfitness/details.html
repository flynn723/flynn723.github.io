WIN Fitness offers individualized fitness & nutrition programs, convenient at home training and insight into today’s leading fitness tips.

Mike Marowitz has been in the fitness industry for 5 years. He began his career after his football career at Kansas Wesleyan University where he also received his BA in Sports Management. Throughout his life in competitive sports, Mike has had the privilege of working with and being trained by some of the best trainers in Orange County. As his passion and knowledge for fitness grew he knew that he had something special to offer people in their fitness journey. In 2013, Mike also decided to push his personal fitness level and compete in 2 NPC Men’s Physique Fitness Competitions. Through this personal experience, he has been able to safely modify his client’s nutrition intake and give them effective tips to enhance their training experience with him.
